calCivil

Civil engineering calculations all in one place

calCivil is an all-in-one civil engineering calculator app designed to simplify your site calculations.

Co-ordinate Distance Calculator

Enter the co-ordinates and it will calculate the distance between them.

Concrete Volume calculator

Enter the shape, dimensions and it will calculate the concrete volume with cement, sand and aggregate quantities.

Unit Convertor

This app converts the value to the desired units.

Brick Quantity Calculator

Enter the wall dimensions, choose brick dimensions and get your number of bricks required

Plaster Quantity Calculator

Enter the wall dimensions, plaster ratio and it will return you the required quantity of each material as well as the total quantity.
